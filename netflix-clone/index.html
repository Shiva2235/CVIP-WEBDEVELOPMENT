  • You can watch Netflix on various devices, and the availability may vary depending on your region. Here are common devices and platforms where you can access Netflix: Smartphones and Tablets: Download the Netflix app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Computers: Access Netflix through web browsers. Visit the Netflix website, log in, and start streaming. Smart TVs: Many smart TVs have the Netflix app pre-installed, or you can download it from the TV's app store.

  • If you want to cancel your Netflix subscription, you can do so by following these general steps. Keep in mind that the specific steps may vary slightly depending on your device and region: On a Computer: Open a web browser and go to the Netflix website. Log in to your Netflix account. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner. Select "Account" from the drop-down menu. In the Account settings, find the "Membership & Billing" section. Click on the "Cancel Membership" button and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation. On a Mobile Device (iOS or Android): Open the Netflix app. Tap the three horizontal lines in the bottom-right corner to open the menu. Select "Account." Under the "Plan Details" section, tap on "Cancel Membership" and follow the prompts to confirm the cancellation.
